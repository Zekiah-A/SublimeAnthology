Creativity - By Hanash

It ushered itself into the dark blue blade
The embodiment of thought, an azure metal
Sometimes red, of quick malice in the swift cut
Othertimes not, a dark sky that speaks not of vengeance
But what. Sometimes it yearns for blood, conflict,
Othertimes honour.
Contained in that sword, that fragment of metal,
Yet freer than the icy bonds it be held within.
