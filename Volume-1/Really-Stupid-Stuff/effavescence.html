Effavescence - By Zekiah

Sounds in physics,
sound at home, waves, longitudional,
transferring through flesh and
bone.

Inescapable sounds of sorrow,
of death at war unravels,
travels at a terrific speed,
faster than the richest steeds,
faster than the poorest slums,
but slower than a firing gun.
You can't run. From these waves,
the rate they vibrate,
is faster than any machine, that you could try make.
No debate.

I don't rate all of this dissipated noise.
From idiots, schizos, so settle down, boys,
and enjoy the sounds, coming straight from my mouth.
Recordings of truths sound different out loud.

Life-giving medium, air, is what sound travels through.
Sound estranged to silence, in deep space's vacuum.
