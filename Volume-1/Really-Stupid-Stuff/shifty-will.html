Shifty Will - By Zekiah

Burrowing into the sun-hidden bush,
Deep in the realm where the sun don’t shine,
Silent, sly, slithering, hushed
As all shifty know it’s a rue; a mere lie.

Cringing into the thicks of it; seemingly harmless.
Straight out of the bush, but back into the darkness.
And right again out, as soon gone in,
Just leaving the marks, where shifty will had been.
