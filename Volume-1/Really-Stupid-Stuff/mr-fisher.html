Mr Fisher - By Zekiah

Mr Fisher, sat down in the chair,
as he scrolled through each student's work,
Matthew, Mark, Jacob, John,
then as his eyes fell onto one, he starts to smirk.
Back arched, body lurched,
over the desks, he started sweating.
Herr Fisher's favourite student, and as
he read it, he started undressing.

They clearly did this on the bus,
you can see the text getting larger,
It doesn't matter for Herr Fisher though,
as each paragraph is getting harder,
to read.

Screech. A loud sound from the back of
the class.
The FBI, Police and army marched,
right into the room,
and started to arrest,
this crazy old man,
completely undressed,

Mr Fisher the pedo,
Mr Fisher the creep,
don't worry, he'll be back in school again,
next week.
