Carbo-Hydron of death - By Zekiah

C666H1334 Hydrocarbon of DEATH,
a long, viscous gooey oozy liquid, smaller than a
hair on your head.
C333H666 alkene of life, saviour of
hope, restorer of good, holier than thou,

So next time you ride down the bitumen road,
look out for the Hydrocarbon of Death, as it
hath foretold, an impending doom doth awaits,
so stop that car, slam on the breaks,
and pray, that the hydrocarbon of life
will appear, a fate avoided, oh so near.

Yet if it doesn't, you have no options left,
you may have escaped the car crash,
but not the sniper aimed at your head.
