Skyfall - By Zekiah

The air is becoming denser,
the sky is falling,
the carbon particulates of pollution
are becoming solid.
It's plain appalling.

"Chicken little, more like chicken spittle!"
The vulgar American interrupted
"If the sky comes down"
well,
"We'll just shoot it up."
He turned red, and clenched his throat,
as barley dust and solid oat started to choke
him.

Dying, he pulled out the dual barrel shotgun,
broad and tall, from the pouch at his side.
Bang, Bang, Bang,
He shot at the sky,
Bang, Bang, Bang,
a seagull dropped dead,
Then landed on the farmer's head.

6 minutes later, the forensics team arrived
to study the body,
"Look at the blood on his head"
"I guess he shot himself"
"Shame what these crazy conspiracies do to a man",
except not really.
