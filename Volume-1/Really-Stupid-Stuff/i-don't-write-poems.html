I don't write POEMS - By Noah

Ruffling in the bushes is a creature
A creature baffling, bad and anger
Anger leaving the face of The earth
Earth a desolate planet of death
Death and more death spreading like

Like butter on bread, wholemeal bread
Bread not white, but brown and full
Full of fibre and colour not
Not false, false, false information
Information dense and decrepid, yet young
Young, as young as an old man

Man, I wish I could taste it
It tastes like acid, feels like sunlight
Sunlight shining into the brain of a stain
Stains projected off the weed of a killer
Killer auxins spread out on the shady side
Side with me if you want to live in this world

Worlds collide in the swirling infinite
Infinite, calisthetic, MEAT, alpha lives on
On as I turn on my Mac and leave
Leaving it open on my desk, forever alone
Alone like Axeli.
