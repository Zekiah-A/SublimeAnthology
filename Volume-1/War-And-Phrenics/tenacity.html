Tenacity - By Zekiah

For the first day my eyes bisected every inch of your
personage, I gave you my energy, my all, my heart
my blood, my toil, my gall. Yet, it never returned, no, your
etheral glow, shut out by a blizzard of snow,
my body dropping, as my mind freezes, my fingers numbing
an epiphany.

I pull AKs; now you run,
bullets ripping through you, love,
you didn't give me shit,
It's no mistake.

Two weeks later,
I spit,
on yor grave.
