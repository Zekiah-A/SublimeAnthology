Bamitz - By Zekiah

Curled up into a circle,
a quick, slick momentary trick,
probably followed by a hit or flick
Yet above the waist,
a grave twisting of God’s rules
Objection!
Last mistake; the Sublime appals.

Summoned up to the bright void of darkness,
like some kind of sun stunned wasteland,
a senate curs’d judgement day.
Pleas to be released of your unattoned sins,
your only escape, and
for the jury it’s a mockery, a game

There’s a chance of survival, yet it’s thin
thinned like the eyes of the victim when you performed your trick
How you wish it were penetrated, or
Just a few centimetres more below the hip

Fate lies near to the knuckle, here and now,
or maybe slightly inbetween,
Begging for some invisible-damned sicko’s reprimand,
A reality that could only be orchestrated within Axel’s dream.
