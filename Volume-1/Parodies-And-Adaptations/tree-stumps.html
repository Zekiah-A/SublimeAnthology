Tree Stumps - By Angelos

On another occasion, I charge behind a van,
To hide from drag, that increases with speed squared.
But one of the workers places cones on the road,
Should I stop, possibly go

And one of them springs out the van and legs it up the tree,
Their welding chainsaws, the first twig is seared!

Sawdust! Sawdust! Slower boy!

They’re dissecting the trunk, the tree’s insides are out,
They volleyed and thundered here and now,
My reckless head in his reckless hands
