Ink - By Bushi

It burns into the mind,
A simple line of black.
Torn at the edges, the
simple paper is a
prison to my mind.

Cruel words spoken,
A dark abyss of
Fractured linearity
The black splodges can,
Only lead to one place.

Then to blue
Clumped bunches
of agonies, azure
Stained in an ocean,
Battering the paper,

Navy line of weathered
Wars takes it's toll.

Dramatically Cuts of paper,
Shattered reveal the,
Former Visage : Hidden
In blood.

Ink, it's like Blood isn't it.
Frantically pumped out of
The pen nib. Or my veins.

The paper is a beautiful picture
of Scarlet.
The cut paper morphs into
Skin.
All flesh is paper.
The tales of death cut into
Skin

I write onto the paper
And arteries burst.

To take a life.
