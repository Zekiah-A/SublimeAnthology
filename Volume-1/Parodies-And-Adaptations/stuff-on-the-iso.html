Stuff On The Iso - By Zekiah

We know nothing, we build our saunas broad.
Broad and tall, just like the reptillians that roam the earth,
and the white froths that beat at our lagoons.

Huawei has never threatened us,
yet we know that they lie beneath,
in colossal chrome canisters of crap; a natural home -

Ready to summon a storm, of sedimentary sewage and
dead fish bones.

Produced by us.

Carbon particulates rain down on the grilli, simmering,
smoking, and burning through the sausages.
Rain turns yellow as each sceptic tank blows full,
reducing the iso to a mound of crap and flies.

Yet,
it is a giant seagull which we still fear?
