Guts / Griffith

Guts (noun) -

The protagonist in the popular manga series, ext:beserk, known for overcoming great hardships, and being a strong fighter.


Griffith (noun) -

An antagonist within the popular manga series beserk, who is shown as committing horrific acts, yet many online communities present the character as misunderstood, and claim that the character 'did nothing wrong'.
