Bibbilance (noun) -

A fictitious word referring to a curse / omen of bad luck, in order to issue retribution for a wrongdoing. A bamitz is often used to display a mildly negative event, such as losing your pencil case, after filling someone's water bottle with fountain pen ink, and a bamitz issued by someone onto another is only of the same intensity as their original action, however punishments from a being such as the Sublime can be much worse.
