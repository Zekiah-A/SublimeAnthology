The Tale of Major Korean - By Bushi

I once knew a man, a sellsword , traveller, wanderer,
His name was Major Korean, and now is recounted his tale of woe;

        It was summer of 1923, or whenever it was auf der große streets,
        I recognised a shadow, that lurked in the corner,
        Stacks of money, all but useless
        But the person I saw, said his name was,
        A "Major Korean".

I heard nothing more, until the second great war;
A rogue soldier, not a nazi in the war,
Travelled to a land unknown, fought to save many lives
Grasped only a sword, an iron carved sword,
Stained with valour, heart of woe.
The warrior who fought for no country, only to save the others
Named Major "Korean"

Then I saw, at the door of the gates by the court,
Tried for crimes, against the law, sat Major Korean,
Crimes that were not crimes, such as speaking against a dictatorship.
One of the few people I knew, was Major Korean, who was an
Anti-racist.

        But where is he now, what land does he lie,
        A slayer, what became of the man, known as
        Major Korean?

This fellow, who was not even Korean, went under that alibi,
For what, to protect which truths? Hide a life of honour?
That person who defeated die Leute, tried end racism,
His fate unknown, Major Korean, the Anti-Racist.

T'was a dark day, when I realised, he had vanished,
Off the face of the Earth.
Die Geselschaft, a great enemy, back-stabbed that warrior
What cave, creek or crevice did he hide, branded as a
Threat to society?

        I grasped a sword and trained, under the waterfall in the
        Dead of the night, stealing my heart to be full of resolve,
        To act out justice, restore that myth's honour.
        The Mythical Major Korean.

In those old days, when Stresemann reigned,
How we'd all think,
I have Weimar, my republic,
How I'd want the Kaiser to be King!
But now, it seems, we were wrong...
The old days, age of slayers are now far gone.
Hoonter, Reece, Noct, Major Korean, Speedwagon, Zekiahepic, Barir
Those times, am besten Zeit
Die Zukunft war nicht zu toll...

But the tale does not end, no- not the take of Major Korean,
Borne in 1066, borne of blood, brother in battle to the slayers,
The un-racist könig.
War is coming,a great plight, so, he took up th' mantle
One last time.

A final clash of steel, iron against iron,
Last march of the catgirl slayers, before stepping into new dawn...
Major Korean fought valiantly, but comrade after comrade fell,
Bloodshed blood lets more mo'r blood
And the ground bleeds red,
It was then when he said, as the dawn of the next day set;
Tears in his eyes, tired, could not fight anymore,

I am sorry, Bushi, Noct, Martin Ligations, Swexys, Gundalf,
Barir, Zekiahepic, Speedwagon, Samurai of the Spear, Dark Juice,
Juloo, Delta. Solaire, Solar System Man, Reece and all fellow
Other Slayer;

        In order, to defeat this foe, and restore this honour...
        I must become racism...
        It must be done, thank you everyone...
        This truly was an adventure, wasn't it?

Then with one final attack, a desperate last resort, a desperate last stand.
Major Korean unleashed his last attack, his final strike...
All the enemies were slain, at the cost of Major Korean's sacrifice,
With a final smile, he faded away, finally at peace, at his end.

        This was the tale of Major Korean.
