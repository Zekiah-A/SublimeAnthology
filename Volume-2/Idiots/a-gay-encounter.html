A gay encounter - By Zekiah

Each time I walk through the woke side of city,
4 times out of 1, I hear an opinion so
shitty.

Blue dyed hair, chubby fingers, short
stance. The clown of the town,
inactive activists rant.

And as I pass by the crossing, I stare down the street,
same story, as always,
black square on her screen.

As you barge past her large body,
made a mistake you'll regret,
as you smack
their/her/his/xer vegan soymilk starbucks latte
on the road.

The concorde blasts quiet, compared to her
shrill shriek, RACISM, SEXISM, HOMOPHOBIA,
MYSOGONY,
prepare to shut your brain off, as she cries

Think about her poor voice, when she's
dead at 55.

Think about about the blue dye washing over her saggy
fuselage, as the wind starts blowing.

Think about the poor flakes, when it
starts snowing.

Then question how can someone who posts so
much positivity and peace on instagram, facebook, and even her tweets
be the most vulgar-mouthed bitch,
as she rolls down the street.
