We live in a dictatorship - By Angelos

So this last day of school,
When you’ve aced your last exam,
Forget those lies and rip off your tie,
Tear your school trousers in a pair of ripped jeans,
Put your blazer under the guillotine,
Till it’s hanging strips of blue and red.
Fling your tie against the playground fence,
And scribble on the fabric, in black whiteboard pen,
Hark the Herald, Hail the woods!
Glory to the ‘‘Book of fame’’
- Insert a quote of dignity and inspiration
- Shut up! You Year Heads of abomination
That’s my quote for you eternally doomed,
Don’t like it? I’m fine in the Withdrawal Room.
And meet me up with Mr Statin,
To teach him the fate of OzyAndy.
‘‘Upload a yearbook photo’’
NO is my moto

For Google Classroom’s chock full of lies,
Waffle, waffle in physics and biology,
Mice eat cats and cats eat mice,
"See me at lunch." You forgot to circle that anomaly.
Respiration is just combustion of glucose,
And photosynthesis – just CO2 in a test tube!
Don’t bother writing a single bit more,
See I told you! One out of four!
Your sketchbook's too abstract! Your portrait’s too pale,
Unfortunately, It will all have to be a total fail,
The sun looks too mellow,
You need more yellow.
Does it have to glow?
Skitzgerald said so!
Everything is watched,
Did I run? Did I walk?
Am I sweating or wet, or smell of ammonia?
- Careful, you’ll catch bronchitis and pneumonia!’’
- Fuck off you Spazpole and Martin, your boyfriend!
For none of you are my Mum or Dad.
If you make keeping fit more so of a pain,
We will end up obese and lame. (like you)

Besides:
Those five aside fucking footie fanatics,
For each touch, each goal between the coats and bags – just pedantic,
You accuse me of only my glistening sweat,
But the footie players are the ones, who their seats do wet!
Dem never asked me if this made me puke,
But my mere morning jog, they rebuke.
Democracy is DEAD. Bojo sealed his approval,
His head of a mop and his eyes of ice,
On parliament did he STOMP twice.
SHN – Stay home now! Protect our fantastic NHS!
In your fantasy it lies, you idiot, you clown-punk.
So resign right now and clear your muck,
About your regulations, we don’t give a fuck.
Our life revolves around one CT,
Oh no invalid! Just the T, no C!
Lies, lies, lies, these are all LIES,
But we believe them
Why? For the benefit and satisfaction,
A pointless endeavor.
Of a minuscule fraction!
