Industrial Society - By Hanash

Machine Whirs Like Urea In The
Wind That Carries Society
On It's Cold Castle In The
Grass.

"I like society" said a man, his
name was "I".
"Society is Potassium Dichromate (IV)"
said a woman whose name was "Society"
"I am Society" said an idiot.

They lived in a society, of idiots.
Nature around them whirled into
Sand and there they were.
At a shattered visage, because
all cliffs have a summit.

"Idiots"
