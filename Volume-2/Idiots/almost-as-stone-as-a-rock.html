Almost as stone as a rock - By Zekiah

Es ist schwierig,
sehr schweirig wie ein Stein,
As the walls fall in, and he crumbles into stone,
in the place of his heart: brick,
and his mind: rock,
And as he starts to decompose,
Er hat sehr dick bekommen,
und ist jetzt tot.
OzyAndy weathering at his craggy throne,
by the playground the seagulls sing,
"OzyAndy, King of Kings",
Stone crumbles to rock, and
rock crumbles to dust,
Mother nature cherishes, and licks with age,
Father nature knows, he must do what he must,
Now only a gargoyle, on the lone rock
at land's end,
"Hier liegt ozy-andy"
haben sie gelesen.
