A conclusive thesis on modern day consumerism - By Zekiah

A westener went, up a mountain.
Up the mountain, in search of a truth.
A truth turned to a revelation,
of answers he would not accept,
his only detriment,
and this ingrained prejudice grew and bred,
like that desire for the latest iPhone, must always be so new,
and his greed for money, a rat in the cycle,
what an idiot.
who would've knew?
