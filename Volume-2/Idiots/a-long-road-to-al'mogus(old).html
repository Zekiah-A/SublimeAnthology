A long road to Al'mogus - By Zekiah

Earth and Bones, old sticks and stones,
Just one long road to Al'mogus.
Sandles, seared by the sun-struck path,
oh how I wish I had taken the car.
All for a land, oh so, so far.
Struggling to Al'mogus.

A vulture, flew overhead,
then dropped down dead! Beating
my back like a brick of lead. Yet I
Shouted 'horay', meat for days!
On my way to Al'mogus.

Fingers blue and black, have left the track.
A new track of red, and filled with dread,
I finally fall, and smash my head,
I start dreaming about Al'mogus.

A winding road once lead to a land a far,
of wealth and riches, gold and cars.
Yet barren bodies of idiots lined it's paths,
An American dream of Al'mogus.
