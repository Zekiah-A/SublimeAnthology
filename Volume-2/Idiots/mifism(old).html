MIFISM - By Zekiah

The test is over,
the marking has begun,
"MISS MISS MISSSS
Can I have one mark here
MISS
Please just one",

The class is livid, everyone with
their hands in their ears, sick of
their retarded cry,
"You don't need any more FUCKING
marks, you have grade 9."
You're wasting time, It's just a mistake,
You can't do this in your GCSEs,
You're making us late, to break,
and lunch, and life, and going home,
So respectfully,
Enjoy your grade,
Control your emotions,
Please, just ask after,
It's just a number.

It makes no difference,
Do we care? NO!
