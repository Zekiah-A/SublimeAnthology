Old Man Consequences - By Zekiah

Billious green,
grillious bile,
happy to sad, sad to smile,
knife to skin, wrinkled to cut,
Smile to pain, pain to blood.

Sawing the knife blade, back again,
he pains, but smiles, happy he bled,
Happy in hell, you won't remember his name,
growing seeds with his blood,
as he let it rain.
