Asylum - By Hanash

The man stepped down into the cell, padded walls, blank grey,
Grey as his mind. The man, the automaton in his grey
suit, his grey briefcase. And then he accepted his seat.
the last of his will tied itself into the electric chair,
the matte, grey wool bluntly smitten with nothing. Nothing,
Creativity had been sent to die and it would.
The blank walls, the blank walls not even grey entombed the
faceless, shameless man evermore.
