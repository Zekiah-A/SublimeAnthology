Suspended in H - By Zekiah

Bring a knife, get in a fight,
bully, beat up, call racial slurs,
"Dogs and cats are on Chinese plates",
"Muslims are good at crashing planes"
All nothing compared to "Hail H".
The most egregious statement to touch the page,
or plan of any yearbook.

Hail Hunna,
Hail and Snow,
According to the teacher,
all a NO. The only possible
meaning of these words,
could be "Heil H-tler"

And as the teacher read this out,
She stood right up, and decided to shout.
"Sieg Heil", the teacher screamed,
then shot her eyes around the class,

"You heard someone
say something mildly
offensive!"

So you're the racist!

W.R.
